NewElec Design Outline & Charateristics

SIMPLE DESIGN

NewElec relays are engineered to provide users an easy installation and ease of access to relay settings parameters. Every relay includes built-in current transfromers. As such, one can extend the relay amperage range by using interposing current transformers. In those relays that feature earth leakge protection, the relays also include an earth leakage core. All trip fault indications are easily seen and with the exception of overload faults has to be reset by an authorised electrician. The relays are designed for use on class F insulated motors.
